## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: Pages Not Indexing After 7 Days
**Solution:**
- Check robots.txt not blocking
- Verify canonical URLs correct
- Request indexing again
- Check for manual actions in GSC

### Issue: No Impression Increase
**Solution:**
- Give it 2-3 weeks minimum
- Check keyword competitiveness
- Consider adding more internal links
- Ensure schema is validated

### Issue: High Impressions, Low CTR
**Solution:**
- Improve meta descriptions
- Test different title formats
- Add FAQ schema for snippet capture
- Check SERP competitors' titles
